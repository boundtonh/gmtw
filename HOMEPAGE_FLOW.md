# Homepage Flow — Green Mountain Tableworx

## Page Philosophy
The homepage has one job: make someone feel like they've found the place that makes *the* table they've been imagining. Premium, handcrafted, serious. The CTA should feel inevitable, not pushy.

---

## Proposed Homepage Section Flow

### 1. Hero
**Full-viewport hero** with dramatic full-bleed photo of a flagship live edge dining table in a luxury interior.

- Headline (IvyPresto, large): *"Where the forest meets your table."* (or TBD with client)
- Sub: Alwyn body — "Custom live edge, river & ocean tables. Handcrafted in New England."
- Primary CTA: **"Get an Instant Estimate"** → `/estimate`
- Secondary CTA: **"Explore Our Work"** → scrolls to gallery section
- Subtle location pill: "📍 Showrooms in Concord, NH & Smithfield, RI"
- Overlay: dark-to-transparent gradient from bottom, ensures text legibility

---

### 2. Signature Intro / Brand Statement
**Short, centered editorial block** — near-white section (`gmt-offwhite`)

> "Every piece begins with a single slab of wood — its grain, its history, its character. We build around that."

- 2–3 sentences max, IvyPresto italic
- No product names, no prices — just brand voice
- Small GMT green rule above

---

### 3. The Three Pillars (Live Edge / River / Ocean)
**Dark forest green section** (`gmt-forest`) — introduces the bread-and-butter products

3 large cards (or side-by-side panels), each:
- Full-bleed photo (hover reveals slight overlay + arrow)
- Title: "Live Edge Tables" / "River Tables" / "Ocean Tables"
- 1-line description
- → links to respective category page

Layout: 3-column on desktop, stacked on mobile

---

### 4. "Every Room. Every Purpose." — All Furniture Types
**Light mist section** (`gmt-mist`) — introduces full product range

Subtitle label: "WHAT WE MAKE"

Grid of product type cards — each card:
- Clean product photo
- Category name
- Starting price badge (or "Inquire Within")
- Subtle arrow link

Categories: Dining Tables · Conference Tables · Coffee Tables · Benches · Console Tables · Countertops · Bars · Shelves & Mantels · Wall Art

---

### 5. Process / How It Works
**White/cream section** — builds trust, reduces hesitation

Subtitle: "HOW A CUSTOM PIECE COMES TO LIFE"

4 steps (horizontal on desktop, vertical on mobile):
1. **Estimate** — Start online in minutes with our estimator
2. **Consult** — We refine your vision together (in-showroom or remote)
3. **Craft** — Handbuilt by our artisans in New England
4. **Deliver** — In-room delivery anywhere in New England

Each step: number in GMT green, bold title, 1-sentence description

---

### 6. Featured Work / Portfolio Gallery
**Dark section or light** — TBD based on photography

Subtitle: "RECENT WORK"
Masonry or horizontal scroll gallery — 6–8 photos
"View More" CTA → future gallery page or Instagram

---

### 7. Table Bases Callout
**Accent section** — quick callout since bases are their own nav item

Short headline: "The Right Base Completes the Piece"
3–4 base thumbnail previews
→ "Explore Table Bases" link

---

### 8. Testimonials
**Cream/offwhite section**

Subtitle: "WHAT OUR CUSTOMERS SAY"
2–3 testimonial cards: quote + name + piece ordered
Client to supply quotes

---

### 9. Showroom Strip
**GMT Green background** — high contrast, location-focused

"Visit Us In Person"
Two location cards side by side:
- Concord, NH — address, phone, hours
- Smithfield, RI — address, phone, hours
Both with → "Get Directions" links
Mention: "In-room delivery available across New England"

---

### 10. Estimate CTA — Full Section
**Dark forest green** — final conversion moment

Large IvyPresto headline: "Start Your Custom Piece Today"
Body: "Use our online estimator to spec your table — dimensions, wood species, epoxy color, edge style, and base. We'll be in touch within 24 hours."
CTA button: **"Get an Instant Estimate →"**

- No section should feel like filler — each earns its place
- Mobile-first: all sections must look great at 375px width

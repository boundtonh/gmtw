# Brand — Green Mountain Tableworx

## Identity
**Name:** Green Mountain Tableworx
**Abbreviation:** GMT
**Tagline:** TBD — client to confirm

## Brand Feel
High-end furniture — think RH (Restoration Hardware) meets Vermont craft studio. Premium, unhurried, handmade. The wood is the hero. Every piece is one of a kind.

## Voice & Tone
- Confident, not boastful
- Craft-forward — reference the material, the process, the maker
- Warm but not casual — this is a considered purchase
- No exclamation points
- No "amazing", "stunning", "beautiful" — show, don't tell
- Short sentences. Let the imagery carry the weight.

## Logo
- GMT Green (#009440) is present in the logo — it is the primary focal color across the site
- Logo files: client to supply SVG + PNG versions
- Dark and light variants needed (for use on dark and light backgrounds)

## Photography Direction
- Wood grain, epoxy texture, finished surfaces — close, detailed, dramatic light
- Raking/side light preferred — shows grain and depth
- No stock photography — all images must be GMT's own work
- Alt text: always include wood species, style, and approximate dimensions

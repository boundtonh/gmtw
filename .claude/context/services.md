# Services & Products — Green Mountain Tableworx

## Product Categories

### Live Edge Tables `/live-edge-tables`
Hub category — organic raw wood edge preserved from the natural slab. Most popular style. Can be dining, conference, coffee, console, bench, or bar configuration.

### River Tables `/river-tables`
Epoxy "river" running through the center of the slab. Available in any color — blues, greens, blacks, whites. High visual impact. Related to ocean tables.

### Ocean Tables `/ocean-tables`
Coastal-inspired epoxy design — flowing, layered ocean aesthetic. Lighter and more airy than river tables. Related to river tables.

### Dining Tables `/dining-tables`
Starting at $1,500. Highest search volume category. Available as live edge, river, or ocean style.

### Conference Tables `/conference-tables`
Starting at $2,500. B2B angle — offices, boardrooms, meeting rooms. Larger dimensions. Highest stated starting price.

### Coffee Tables `/coffee-tables`
Starting at $500. Entry-level price point. Good "first custom piece" framing.

### Benches `/benches`
Starting at $500. Dining bench, entryway, bedroom, mudroom configurations.

### Console Tables `/console-tables`
Starting at $750. Entryway, hallway, behind-sofa use cases.

### Countertops & Island Tops `/countertops-island-tops`
Inquire within. Kitchen and island integration. Complex pricing based on dimensions.

### Standalone Bars & Bar Tops `/bars-bar-tops`
Inquire within. Home bar, entertaining spaces, man cave.

### Floating Shelves & Mantels `/floating-shelves-mantels`
Inquire within. Fireplace mantels and floating shelf systems.

### Wall Art & Signage `/wall-art-signage`
Inquire within. Wood art, branded signage, personalized pieces.

### Table Bases `/table-bases`
Standalone nav item. Multiple base styles available — client to supply names and photos. Shown in thumbnail format in the estimate wizard.

## Customization Options (applies to most products)
- Wood species (client to supply full list)
- Dimensions (length × width in inches)
- Table shape: Rectangle, Circle, Oval, Boat
- Epoxy color (or none)
- Background color
- Edge style: Live Edge, Straight, Bevel, C-Shaped, Lake Shaped, Chiseled
- Surface finish: Full Gloss, Full Matte, Matte Wood w/ Glossy Resin
- Engraving: Yes / No
- Table base selection

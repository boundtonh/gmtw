## Keywords to Avoid
- "cheap" / "affordable" / "budget" — conflicts with premium positioning
- "fast" / "quick" — custom orders take time, don't overpromise lead time
- Generic: "wood table", "furniture store" — too broad, no local intent

## Usage Rules
- Primary keyword appears in: `<h1>`, `<title>`, first paragraph of page copy, at least one `<h2>`
- Secondary keywords woven naturally into body copy — never stuffed
- Location terms (NH, RI, New England) appear at least twice per category page
- Never repeat the exact same keyword phrase more than 3 times per page
